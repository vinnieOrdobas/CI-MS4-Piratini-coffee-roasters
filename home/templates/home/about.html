About us

When we first imagined P&Co., Bruce and I were baristas in a busy speciality coffee shop, and we knew what it take to brew good coffee: passion and dedication. We are also passionate about growing plants and the natural world, so we decided to create our own roastery with beans produced only by high-quality, organic farmers who care for nature as much as we do: we discovered that many people thought like us, that respect for the planet has to be the top priority.

P&Co. was born from the idea that coffee is not a commodity, but a living product: just like wine, coffee is a complex beverage that takes its wonderful attributes from the land where the coffee tree has grown.
